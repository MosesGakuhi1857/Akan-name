About Akan Naming

Akan people from Ghana frequently name their children after days of the week they were born in,below is a list of namings;

  • monday - Kwado
  • tuesday - Kwabena
  • wednesday - Kwaku
  • thursday - Yaw
  • friday - Kofi
  • saturday - Kwame
